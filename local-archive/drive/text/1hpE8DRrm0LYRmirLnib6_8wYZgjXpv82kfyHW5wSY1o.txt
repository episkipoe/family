8/31/2002
It’s my habit to walk around the house while brushing my teeth.
Today I absentmindedly carried the tube of toothpaste with me.
It strikes me how oddly out of place it looks in rooms other than the bathroom.
And from this observation we make a decision:  toothpaste needs to get out more.
So we head to a park in Gurnee.
